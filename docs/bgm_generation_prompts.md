# Vampire Survivors Style Chiptune BGM Prompt

Create a seamless looping 8-bit chiptune music, NES/Famicom style, for a Vampire Survivors-like space roguelike game. Warm, non-harsh sound.

## Style
- **130-140 BPM**
- **NES 2A03/APU** sound chip style, but warm and smooth
- **Soft square wave** (12.5% duty) for lead - less harsh than 50%
- **Triangle wave** for bass and melody fill
- **Gentle pulse wave** for harmonies
- **Subtle noise** for light percussion
- **No reverb, minimal effects** but soft, rounded sound
- Short notes, simple memorable melodies
- 8-bar or 16-bar loop

## Melody
- Catchy, peaceful 8-bit melody
- Vampire Survivors' driving feel but softer
- Build tension gradually
- Space theme: high dreamy notes for stars, warm low tones for engines

## Instruments (Soft NES Style)
- **Pulse1**: Lead melody (square, 12.5% duty - softer)
- **Pulse2**: Harmony (square, 12.5% duty)
- **Triangle**: Bass + melody fill
- **Noise**: Light kick/snare (subtle, not harsh)

## Sound Design
- **Rounded, warm 8-bit** - avoid harsh piercing tones
- Use lower volumes on pulse waves
- Occasional vibrato on long notes
- Simple patterns, never overwhelming
- Gentle dynamics

## Technical
- WAV, 44.1kHz/16-bit
- 30-60 seconds loop
- -10dB volume
- Original only

## Negative
- No 50% duty square waves (too harsh)
- No piercing high frequencies
- No reverb, delay, or effects
- No modern synths or pads
- No human vocals
- No loud/distorted sounds
